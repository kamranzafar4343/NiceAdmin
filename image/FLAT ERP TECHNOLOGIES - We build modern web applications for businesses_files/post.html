Here is the complete SCHOOL MANAGEMENT APPLICATION you will ever need to effectively manage your school in single or across multiple locations. It supports Day and Boarding facilities. It is designed to streamline and automate various administrative tasks in Nursery, Primary, Secondary and Colleges. 1. Student Information Management: The application allows administrators to maintain a comprehensive database of student information, including personal details, contact information, academic records, ID cards, testimonial and medical information.
2. Attendance Tracking: It has a module for tracking student attendance, allowing teachers to mark attendance easily and generate attendance reports.
3. Grade and Transcript Management: Teachers can record and manage student grades, calculate GPA, and generate report cards. The application also supports the creation and maintenance of student transcripts.

5. Communication and Collaboration: It enables seamless communication between teachers, parents, and students through messaging module, announcements, and event calendars. It also offers parent portal access to keep parents informed about their child's progress and school-related updates.
6. Library Management: This module helps in managing the school library, including cataloging books, tracking borrowings and returns, and generating reports on library usage.
7. Finance and Fee Management: The application include modules for managing financial aspects, such as fee collection, invoice generation, expense tracking, and financial reporting.
8. Staff Management: It allows administrators to maintain staff records, track attendance, assign roles and responsibilities, and manage payroll.
9. Data Analytics and Reporting: The application generates various reports and analytics, such as student performance reports, attendance summaries, financial statements, and other customized reports.
